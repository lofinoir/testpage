About Me

My name is Giovanny, but everybody calls me G.

I am a professional editorial and commercial photographer. I specialize on in-studio and on-location commercial advertising assignments, high-end architectural interiors, photojournalism, and reportage. I have photographed critters, places and human beings in multiple countries across four continents.

Completed a Fine Arts Specialization in Graphic Design Program from California Arts Institute while receiving Art History, Architectural Design, and Technical Drafting education as part of the BAMFA Program Curriculum.

I can and have covered photo assignments in just about every type of environment, event, and situation you can imagine. Places with minus 14-degree cold in arctic locations to sub-Saharan and remote villages in 3rd world countries.

I have photographed remote zones that westerners seldom venture into and found the local people to be the most hospitable, humble, and welcoming human beings I have ever met.

I make photographs with the intent to "get them as close to completed in-camera"; which stems from learning photography on film and not relying on retouching to make the photo acceptable; never trust a photographer who says; "I'll fix it in Photoshop it later”
